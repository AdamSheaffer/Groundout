Log Your Trips

Ground Out is a great tool for baseball fans who are interested in seeing every major league ballpark in America. Track your progess, rate each park, and document the most memorable parts of your trip as you travel across the country. Once finished, you'll have a complete catalog of your entire experience.

Find Tickets

Ground Out makes searching for tickets easy. Just click on the team you'd like to visit next, and we'll find you the cheapest tickest on the web to every game for the rest of the season. Not sure which team or park to hit next? Check out other users' park ratings and what they had to say.
